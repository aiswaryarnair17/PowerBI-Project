# Customer Churn & Revenue Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of customer churn and revenue risk. It helps businesses identify high-risk customer segments, understand churn patterns, and quantify revenue loss.

---

## Key Features
- **KPI Cards:** Total Customers, Churn Rate (%), Total Revenue, Revenue Lost
- **Churn by Contract Type:** Pie chart showing churn distribution across monthly, one-year, and two-year contracts
- **Churn by Customer Tenure:** Bar chart highlighting churn based on tenure groups
- **Churn: Senior vs Non-Senior:** Pie chart comparing churn between senior and non-senior customers
- **Revenue Lost by Payment Method:** Horizontal bar chart showing revenue loss by payment method
- **Interactive Filters:** Contract type, Gender, Tenure group

---

## Key Insights
- Customers with **tenure below 6 months** have the highest churn rate  
- **Monthly contract customers** churn significantly more than long-term contracts  
- **Senior citizens** have higher churn tendency  
- Certain payment methods (e.g., electronic checks) contribute more to revenue loss
